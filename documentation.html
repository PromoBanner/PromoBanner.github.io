PromoBanner Documentation

Everything you need to get started with PromoBanner for WHMCS.

Easy Activation

Toggle the display of your promotional banners directly from the WHMCS admin panel.

Installation

Follow these simple steps to install and activate PromoBanner on your WHMCS site.

  • Download the latest version of the PromoBanner module from the Releases section.
  • Unzip the downloaded file and upload the PromoBanner folder to your WHMCS /modules/addons/ directory.
  • Log into your WHMCS admin area. Navigate to System Settings > Addon Modules.
  • Locate PromoBanner in the list of addon modules and click the "Activate" button.
  • After activation, click "Configure" to access and customize the module settings according to your preferences.

Configuration

Customize PromoBanner to fit your needs with our easy-to-use settings.

  • Banner Enabled: Toggle the display of your promotional banner.
  • Banner Text: Customize the text displayed on the banner.
  • Banner Background Color: Choose a color for the banner background.
  • Text Color: Select the color of the text displayed on the banner.
  • Hover Color: Define a color for the text when hovered over. This will change the text color to make it stand out on mouseover.
  • Link Underline on Hover: Enable or disable the underline on the banner link when hovered over. Provides a visual cue to users that the banner is clickable.
  • Promo Code: Specify a promo code to be included in the banner's link, encouraging users to take advantage of special offers.

Scheduling

Control when your promotional banner is displayed with our scheduling options.

  • Schedule Start: Enter the start date and time for when you want your banner to begin displaying. Use the format YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS.
  • Schedule End: Enter the end date and time for when you want your banner to stop displaying. Use the format YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM:SS.

Note: Leaving the scheduling fields blank will keep the banner visible indefinitely, or until you choose to manually disable it.
