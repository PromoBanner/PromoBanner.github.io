Features

Dynamic Domain Support

Automatically adjusts banner links to your WHMCS installation's domain.

Customizable Appearance

Full control over text boldness, color, and hover effects for seamless integration.

Easy Activation

Toggle the display of your promotional banners directly from the WHMCS admin panel.

Installation

Follow these simple steps to install and activate PromoBanner on your WHMCS site.

  • Download the latest version of the PromoBanner module from the Releases section.
  • Unzip the downloaded file and upload the PromoBanner folder to your WHMCS /modules/addons/ directory.
  • Log into your WHMCS admin area. Navigate to System Settings > Addon Modules.
  • Locate PromoBanner in the list of addon modules and click the "Activate" button.
  • After activation, click "Configure" to access and customize the module settings according to your preferences.

Configuration

Customize PromoBanner to fit your needs with our easy-to-use settings.

  • Banner Enabled:Toggle the display of your promotional banner.
  • Banner Text:Customize the text displayed on the banner.
  • Banner Background Color:Choose a color for the banner background.
  • Text Color:Select the color of the text displayed on the banner.
  • Hover Color:Define a color for the text when hovered over.
  • Promo Code:Specify a promo code to be included in the banner's link.
